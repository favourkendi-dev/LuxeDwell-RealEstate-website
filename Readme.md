### Project Title
LUXEDWELL REAL ESTATE WEBSITE
### Project Description
LuxeDwell is a high end  responsive web platform designed for Nairobi’s premier real estate market. The project focuses on bridging the gap between architectural art and functional urban living, providing a bespoke digital experience for high net worth investors and discerning homeowners in prime nodes like Karen, Westlands and Muthaiga.

### Problem Faced

Finding a home in Nairobi is a complex challenge driven by **information asymmetry** and a lack of centralized, verified data. The market is often saturated with "ghost listings" and deceptive photography used by unregulated agents to harvest leads, forcing seekers to navigate a landscape of high stakes verification and informal gatekeepers. This creates a significant "transparency gap," where the physical reality of a property rarely matches its digital advertisement, necessitating a more direct, visual-first approach to bypass traditional bottlenecks and reduce the risk of fraud.

### Problem Solved

I have addressed the pervasive transparency gap in the Nairobi real estate market by developing a digital solution that prioritizes data integrity and user trust. I have engineered a platform that replaces unreliable "ghost listings" and deceptive photography with verified  high-fidelity image galleries allowing seekers to virtually vet properties before committing to a physical visit. Furthermore, I have eliminated the friction of informal gatekeepers by integrating a direct WhatsApp communication channel  ensuring that the journey from discovering a house to securing a home is transparent  efficient and secure.
